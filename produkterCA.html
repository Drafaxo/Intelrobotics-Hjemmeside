UR3 Robotarms

The flexible, lightweight collaborative UR3 cobot weighs only 11 kg, but can lift 3 kg and has 360 degree rotation in all wrists as well as unlimited rotation in all ends. The UR3 robot can be used for many purposes in the manufacturing sectors, from medical devices over printed circuit boards to electronic components.

As the latest product in our series of collaborative robots, UR3e offers future-proofing with collaborative innovations, a user experience that puts people at the center and an ecosystem for any application. Make ambitions into results by changing the way things are done, with the most flexible automation platform.

With our patented technology, users without programming experience can quickly set up collaborative robots and operate them through intuitive 3D visualization. Simply move the robot arm and display the movement it needs to perform, or press the arrow keys on the easy-to-use, touch screen.
